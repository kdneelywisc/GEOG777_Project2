Welcome!


We're glad you chose to visit State Forest State Park.

This app is designed to help you find your way around the park. You can search for trails or your favorite scenic views in the search bar, use the filter to see what park amenities are open for winter and much more. Our favorite tool gives you the ability to submit moose sightings! Just use click on the pencil icon to bring up the edit tool. From here you can submit locations that you have spotted a moose or edit your submissions in case you made a mistake.
